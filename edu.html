Education

My educational journey began with a strong foundation at St. Joseph’s GHS Karukutty, where I completed my SSLC in 2019 with an outstanding score of 97.66%. This solid early education paved the way for my Higher Secondary Education (HSE) at St. Joseph’s HSS Karukutty, where I achieved a remarkable 97.25% in 2021. Motivated by my passion for technology and design, I pursued a B.Tech in Computer Science and Design at the Federal Institute of Science and Technology, Ernakulam. Currently in my fourth year, I maintain a CGPA of 8.79 and have developed a keen interest in web development, coding, and leadership. My academic experiences have provided me with both technical knowledge and the ability to work effectively in team settings, and I am excited to apply these skills to real-world challenges in the future.
